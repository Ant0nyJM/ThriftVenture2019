What is Subsequence?

  • First, should understand the difference between substring and subsequence
  • Consider two strings "ABCD" and "BCDE". Substrings common to both strings are BC,BCD,B,C,D,CD
  • Every substring is a subsequence , but not vice versa.
  • In this example ,Common subsequences other than the above substrings : BD
  • LCS is the Longest Subsequence common to both of the strings.There could be more than one LCS.In this example its BCD
  • Consider ABCDE and AXYVEY, here AE is a subsequence ,its also LCS
  • Consider AXYCV and AXCYV. Here more than one LCS ,that is LCS set=(AXYV and AXCV)
  • Fibonacci numbers start with index zero that is f(0)=0 f(1)=1 f(2)=1 f(3)=2 f(4)=3 f(5)=5 f(6)=8 and so on
